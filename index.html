SECRET GARDEN, THE COURT YARD, MERSHAM-LE-HATCH, HYTHE
ROAD, ASHFORD, TN25 5NH / TN25 6NH

How To Get There

BY CAR:
FROM LONDON: Follow the M20, leaving junction 10, take 2nd exit onto the A20 Hythe Road. Go straight over little roundabout next to a Tesco. Follow A20 for approx. 1.6 miles. The venue is on the left hand side, you will see a sign for The Court Yard.
FROM FOLKESTONE: Follow the M20, leaving at junction 11, take 3rd exit onto the A20. Follow the road for approx. 4 miles. You will go through the village of Sellindge. The venue is on the right hand side, you will see a sign for The Court Yard.
Sat navs do not always send you to the right place, this is why there are two postcodes.

BY TRAIN:
The nearest train station is Ashford International, which is approx. a 15 minute car journey away (5 miles).

BY BUS:
Number 10/10A - Ashford to Folkestone - Nearest Stop is Mersham Turn.

Additional venue info

  • • Most of the terrain at Secret Garden is cobble and gravel - bear in mind when choosing shoes!
  • • Cars can be left in the main cark park overnight and can be picked up from 7:30am onwards the following day. The main gate to the venue is locked from midnight to 7am.
  • • It is recommended you pre book taxis. The latest order for a taxi should be 11:30pm due to the above.
  • • The venue only has two high chairs. Please let us know if you think you will need one.
  • • The bar can take card payments, but the minimum amount is £10 without a 50p surcharge.
